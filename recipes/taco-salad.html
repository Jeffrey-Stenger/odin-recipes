Taco Salad

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Shredded cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Tortilla chips

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef in pan until browned.
  2. Once browned, add the taco seasoning to the beef. Stir in.
  3. Dice tomatoes
  4. Chop up lettuce
  5. Final step: mix all ingredients together in a bowl as desired.
