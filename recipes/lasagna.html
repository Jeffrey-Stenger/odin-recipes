Lasagna

Description

This is a classic Italian dish full of noodles, ground beef, and 2 different kinds of cheeses. It can be made for meat lovers or vegetarians alike. This recipe serves 8-10 people.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmasean Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodes.
  2. Cook ground beef in pan until browned.
  3. Layout out alternating layers of noodes, ground beef, and ricotta.
  4. Place baking dish into oven set at 275 degrees.
  5. Let bake for 45 minutes.
  6. Take out, let cool down, and enjoy!
