Cannoli

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Egg
  • Cooking oil
  • Sugar
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Powdered sugar
  • Chocolate chips
  • Pistachios

Steps

  1. Create dough from flour and water.
  2. Mix egg, ricotta, and sugar in a mixing bowl.
  3. Roll dough into forms with a cannoli tube.
  4. Fry cannoli in a frying pot.
  5. Let cool.
  6. Fill cannoli with ricotta mixture, dip each end into chocolate chips or pistachios as desired.
